# SC-010 — Statement of Claim

**Form Number:** SC-010, Rev. 02/20
**Pages:** 1
**Fillable Fields:** 0 (informational only)
**Category:** Small Claims — Instructions
**Filing Fee:** N/A

## Purpose

This document provides instructions to small claims plaintiffs on how to serve the Statement of Claim on the defendant. It describes three methods of service in order of preference: mail with acknowledgment, certified/registered mail, and sheriff service.

## Key Information Provided

### Service Methods (M.R.S.C.P. 4)

1. **U.S. Mail with Acknowledgment (SC-005)**
   - Mail claim + 2 copies of SC-005 + stamped return envelope to defendant
   - Must receive signed acknowledgment back within 20 days
   - File acknowledgment + claim + $70 fee with court

2. **Registered/Certified Mail**
   - Send by registered or certified mail, restricted delivery, return receipt
   - Defendant signs green card at delivery
   - File claim + green card + fee within 20 days

3. **Sheriff Service**
   - Give original + copy to sheriff's office
   - Sheriff serves defendant and files return of service
   - Costs apply
