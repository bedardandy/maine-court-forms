# CV-FM-201 — Instructions for Service by Alternate Means

**Form Number:** CV-FM-201
**Fillable Fields:** 0
**Category:** Civil/Family — Instructions
**Related Forms:** CV-FM-072, CV-FM-202, CV-172, CV-173

## Purpose

Checklist and instructions for requesting court approval to serve papers by alternate means when the other party cannot be found.

## Governing Law

- **M.R. Civ. P. 4(g) (service by alternate means)**

## Notes

This is an instruction/reference form — no fields to fill. One page. Covers: when to use alternate service, prerequisite efforts (mail, certified mail, sheriff, public databases), required forms (CV-FM-072 motion, CV-FM-202 affidavit, CV-172/173 for publication), and filing steps.
