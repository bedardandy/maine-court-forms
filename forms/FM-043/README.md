## Purpose

Comprehensive financial disclosure required in all Maine family cases involving property division, spousal support, or child support. Both parties must file. Covers real estate, vehicles, personal property, bank accounts, retirement, insurance, business interests, lawsuits, tax history, debts, employment, income, and expenses.
