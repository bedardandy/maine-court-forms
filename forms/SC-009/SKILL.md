# SC-009 — What happens at a Small Claims hearing?

**Form Number:** SC-009, Rev. 02/20
**Pages:** 1
**Fillable Fields:** 0 (informational only)
**Category:** Small Claims — Instructions
**Filing Fee:** N/A

## Purpose

This is an informational document provided to defendants in small claims cases. It explains what happens at a hearing, how to prepare, settlement options, payment obligations if judgment is entered, and appeal rights. No fields to fill — this is served on the defendant along with the Statement of Claim.

## Key Information Provided

1. **Hearing process**: Judge hears plaintiff first, then defendant; no lawyer required
2. **Settlement**: Encouraged before hearing; put agreements in writing
3. **Preparation**: Be on time, bring evidence and witnesses, know your rights
4. **If judgment against you**: Must pay within 30 days
5. **Disclosure hearing**: If you can't pay, court may schedule disclosure hearing
6. **Appeal**: Must be filed within 30 days with filing fee
