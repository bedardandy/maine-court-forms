## AI Hints

- **Confidentiality**: Child protection records are highly confidential.
- **DHHS Involvement**: Department of Health and Human Services is typically a party.
- **ICWA**: Check if Indian Child Welfare Act applies.
- **Docket Number**: Leave blank for new filings; clerk assigns. Enter if responding to existing case.
